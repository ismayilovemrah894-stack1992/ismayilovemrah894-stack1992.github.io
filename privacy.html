Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sellora ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our services, including our Amazon Selling Partner API integration.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information from Amazon's Selling Partner API, including:

  • Amazon Seller account identifiers and credentials (via OAuth/LWA)
  • Order information including buyer shipping addresses
  • Product listing data and inventory levels
  • Pricing information
  • Tracking and fulfillment data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve our dropshipping automation services
  • Process and fulfill orders on your behalf
  • Sync product listings, prices, and inventory between platforms
  • Submit tracking information to Amazon and other marketplaces
  • Provide customer support

3. Amazon Selling Partner API Data

Our application accesses Amazon Selling Partner API data solely to provide the services described in this policy. We access the following data roles:

  • Selling Partner Insights: To view account and performance information
  • Pricing: To manage and automate product pricing
  • Inventory and Order Tracking: To manage orders and inventory
  • Product Listing: To create and manage product listings

We do not sell or share Amazon SP-API data with third parties except as necessary to provide our services (e.g., submitting orders to CJ Dropshipping for fulfillment).

4. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • CJ Dropshipping: To fulfill orders placed through our platform
  • Service providers: Who assist us in operating our platform
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain order and fulfillment data for up to 2 years to support order management and dispute resolution. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access tokens are stored securely and never shared.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke our access to your Amazon account at any time via Seller Central

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
